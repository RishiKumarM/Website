What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is a sustainable method that avoids synthetic chemicals, focusing on natural inputs like compost, crop rotation, and biological pest control.

Benefits:

  • Improves soil fertility and water retention
  • Reduces chemical exposure for farmers and consumers
  • Enhances biodiversity and ecosystem resilience
  • Opens premium markets for organic produce

How to Integrate:

  • Use compost and vermicompost instead of synthetic fertilizers
  • Rotate crops and use companion planting
  • Apply neem oil and natural pest traps
  • Adopt drip irrigation and mulching for water efficiency
  • Join certification programs like NPOP or PKVY
